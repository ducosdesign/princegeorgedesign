Common Questions

How do the hourly blocks work?

Think of it as a "bank" of time for your business. You purchase a block of 4, 10, or 20 hours, and we deduct time as we complete your tasks. We track everything in 15-minute increments so you never overpay for small fixes.

Do my hours ever expire?

Your support hours remain valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. This gives you total flexibility to use them for a big project now or small maintenance tweaks throughout the year.

Why is there no tax charged on my order?

As a CRA Small Supplier, I am not currently required to charge GST or PST. This results in an immediate 12% savings for your business compared to larger agencies.

How fast can you start on my project?

Most tasks are initiated within 24 hours. Once you purchase a block and send your "To-Do" list, you'll receive an estimated completion time. Emergency fixes are always given priority.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you aren't happy with the results of your first support block, we will refund your remaining balance—no questions asked.
