The Service

What tasks can you handle?

We handle everything from small CSS tweaks and plugin configurations to full speed optimizations and Shopify Liquid theme edits. If it involves WordPress or Shopify code, design, or maintenance, we can do it.

Is there a limit to how many tasks I can send?

No. You can send as many tasks as you like. We will work through them one by one or as prioritized by you, deducting the time from your active support block.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

While we aren't a 24/7 call center, we are a local studio that guarantees a response within 24 hours. For businesses in the Prince George area, we are often much faster.

Billing & Time

How is time tracked?

We use professional time-tracking software and record work in 15-minute increments. You will receive a detailed log showing exactly what was done and how much time remains in your block.

Do my hours expire?

Hours are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. This allows you to keep a "bank" of time for emergencies or scheduled updates throughout the year.

Why is there no tax on my invoice?

Prince George Design operates as a CRA Small Supplier. This means we are currently exempt from collecting GST/PST, passing those savings directly to your local business.

Guarantees

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you are not happy with the work performed on your first block, we will refund the remaining balance of your hours, no questions asked.

Will my site go down during work?

We take every precaution, including using staging environments for major changes. For small tweaks, we work during low-traffic periods to ensure your customers have a seamless experience.
